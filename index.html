4TH ANNUAL MONADNOCK TIP-UP TOURNEY

SATURDAY, MARCH 5TH 2016

POOL POND, RINDGE, NH

7:00AM - 3:00PM

  • Registration begins at 7:00am on Pool Pond, bob-house headquarters
  • Entry fee $15 per person
  • Kids 12 & under free
  • Register to be entered for hourly door prizes
  • Fishing starts @ 7:00am
  • Fish registration from 7:00am-3:00pm at bob-house headquarters. All fish must be weighed in alive (practice safe catch & release)
  • Prizes will be awarded to the top 3 places in each fish species (Pickerel, yellow perch, white perch, crappie, and horn pout)
  • Winners will be decided by weight. Tiebreakers will be determined by length
  • 50/50 Raffle, drawn at the end
  • Hourly door prizes throughout the day
  • Prize drawings of all registered participants happen every hour from 7 AM until 3PM. Concessions, t-shirts and 50-50 Raffle tickets will be sold at the Registration Bobhouse. Kids 12 and under can be registered for free.

All proceeds will go to Lacey Martin, a 13 year old girl fighting leukemia. To find out more about how you can help visit her on gofundme.com.

Beneficiary

This year's goal is to raise money to help a young girl fighting leukemia. You can find out more information or get in touch with the family by joining their facebook group or on her gofundme page.

LOCATION

Fishing for charity. It Takes Guts.

We host an annual fishing derby on the first Saturday of March on Pool Pond. The event's purpose is to raise money to help a local family in need. The beneficiary may change from year to year depending on local tragedy or circumstance. Our goal is to gather the community together to help our fellow citizens.
